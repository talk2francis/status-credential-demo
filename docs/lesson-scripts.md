# Lesson Scripts (~1 minute each)

## Lesson 1: What is money?
Money is a coordination technology. We walk through commodity origins → fiat trust → programmable assets. Key beats: scarcity + verifiability, separation of value + medium, programmable rules.

## Lesson 2: How blockchains work
Explain blocks, hashes, distributed consensus. Use 60s elevator pitch: nodes, validators, finality, immutability.

## Lesson 3: Wallets and security
Define seed phrase, private/public keys, hot vs cold storage. Mention threat models and best practices.

## Lesson 4: AMMs and liquidity pools
Illustrate constant product formula, role of LP tokens, fee accrual, price impact.

## Lesson 5: Yield farming
Outline stacking incentives, base APR, bonus token, compounding. Highlight risk management and IL hedging.

## Lesson 6: Mock DCA project
Students run sandbox trades every 4h, log rationale, compare vs lump-sum. Rubric ties to XP.
